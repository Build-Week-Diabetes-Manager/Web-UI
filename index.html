Our Mission

Living A Healthy Life

Our number one goal with the Diabetes Manager is to keep your nutrition, and blood sugar, in order. We take the "guessing" out of the equation. It's been proven that steady blood sugar levels result in longer lasting energy, greater focus, and a healthier system overall. Our mission as a company is to give you the power to input, and monitor, your blood sugar levels during all hours of the day. With your inputs, we can predict your future blood sugars, to keep you in a healthy range, all day, every day.

How it benefits you

Putting your mind at ease

With the use of the Diabetes Manager, you can track your food, as well as the time of consumption, during all hours of the day. In doing so, we can use this information to give you an accurate prediction of your future blood sugar levels.

How does this benefit you?
By having predictions of your blood sugars, up to 60 minutes ahead of time, you have the luxury of having your mind at ease, without fretting over sudden drops, or spikes, in your blood sugar.

How does it work?

As simple as 1, 2, 3!

The process is short, and broken down into 3 simple steps:

  1. Prior to eating a meal, input your food directly into the app. Select the type of food, the amount, and the time of consumption.
  2. Be sure to log any exercise into the tracker. While it will count your daily steps, inputting any heavy amounts of exercise will allow it to provide a more accurate reading.
  3. That's it! Simply log your food, and exercise, and the app will take care of the rest. That way you can send less time doing calculations, and more time enjoying your day... and food!

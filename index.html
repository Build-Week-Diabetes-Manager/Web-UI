Our Mission

Our main goal is to assist diabetic patients to keep track of their blood sugar level over the next 24 hours.
This app will use the patients data to predict of the abnormal blood sugar levels.

How App Works

By collecting patients data, this app will be able to predict the blood sugar levels. If the prediction level is abnormal, it will send the notifications to the patient.
